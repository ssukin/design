MOBILE HEALTH

Mobile Health is a medical kit prototype for third world nations.

Mobile Health uses artificial intelligence, internet of things (IoT) tools, and data integration with online calendars and local news to diagnose patients. Mobile Health can also connect to a global network of doctors.

Created as part of the 2017 Cambridge IDEO Makeathon.

Not pictured: IoT thermometer.
