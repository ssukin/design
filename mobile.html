MOBILE HEALTH

Mobile Health is a medical kit prototype for regions without access to medical care.

Mobile Health uses artificial intelligence, internet of things (IoT) tools, and data integration with online calendars, local news, and a global network of doctors.

Created for the 2017 Cambridge IDEO Makeathon. IoT thermometer not pictured.
